ABOUT US

Pine City Mall is a large shopping centre in Vanderbijlpark, Gauteng, South Africa. It is the largest shopping centre in the region and serves patrons from Vanderbijlpark, Vereeniging, Sasolburg, Sebokeng, Bophelong and Parys.

Address: Cnr. Rossini Boulevard &, Barrage Rd, Vanderbijlpark S. W. 2, Vanderbijlpark, 1911, South Africa

Town: Vanderbijlpark

Country: South Africa

Opened: 2006

Owner: Growth Point

Floor Count: 2(ground level, first level)
